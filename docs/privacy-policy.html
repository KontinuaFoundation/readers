PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: April 15, 2025

ReaderIOS is part of the Kontinua Group. This privacy policy will explain how our organization uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our application.

What data do we collect?

Kontinua collects the following data:

  • Personal identification information if submitted (Email address)

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide Kontinua with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

  • Voluntarily provide feedback on our workbooks via the feedback icon on the app.
  • Use or view our website via your browser's cookies.

How will we use your data?

Kontinua collects your data so that we can:

  • Keep track of users on each workbook
  • Contact you if necessary on feedback provided on our workbooks

How do we store your data?

Kontinua securely stores your data on encrypted cloud servers with industry-standard security measures in place, including firewalls, access controls, and regular security audits.

Kontinua will keep your email address for 12 months. Once this time period has expired, we will delete your data by permanently removing it from our active servers and backup systems.

What are your data protection rights?

Kontinua would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access - You have the right to request Kontinua for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  • The right to rectification - You have the right to request that Kontinua correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Kontinua to complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure - You have the right to request that Kontinua erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing - You have the right to request that Kontinua restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing - You have the right to object to Kontinua's processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability - You have the right to request that Kontinua transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:

  • Email: feedback@kontinua.org
  • Or write to us: Kontinua Group, 1234 Tech Way, San Francisco, CA 94103, USA

What are cookies?

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

How to manage cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.

Privacy policies of other websites

The Kontinua website contains links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

Changes to our privacy policy

Kontinua keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this web page. This privacy policy was last updated on April 15, 2025.

How to contact us

If you have any questions about Kontinua's privacy policy, the data we hold on you, or you would like to exercise one of your data protection rights, please do not hesitate to contact us.

  • Email us at: feedback@kontinua.org
  • Or write to us at: Kontinua Group, 1234 Tech Way, San Francisco, CA 94103, USA

How to contact the appropriate authority
